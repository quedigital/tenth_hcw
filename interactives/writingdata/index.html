Writing Data to RAM

To write a bit, tap the address line. Wait for the transistors to close. Tap the data line of the bit you want to write.

Reading Data from RAM

To read a bit, tap the address line to close the transistors. Capacitors with bits stored in them will discharge them out through their data lines.
